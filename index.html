About me

I am currently a postdoc working with Prof. Gesualdo Scutari at the Industrial Engineering school at Purdue University. I joined Purdue in September 2019 as a Lillian Gilbreth Postdoctoral fellow working with Prof. Gesualdo Scutari and Prof. Guang Cheng after graduating with a PhD in Electrical Engineering from the KTH Royal Institute of Technology. During my PhD I focused on decentralized algorithms and their applications to cyber-physical systems. Currently, I am designing algorithms that solve statistical learning problems in a decentralized fashion, and developing the theory to understand which computational, communication and data trade-offs we face when solving decentralized statistical learning problems.
